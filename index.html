Our Craft

Why each piece feels different
in your hand

The Palette 01

Plants. Minerals. Nothing else.

Indigo leaves, madder root, pomegranate peel, myrobalan, iron-jaggery, and alum. A short, honest list — the same one Indian artisans have used for centuries.

The Process 02

30 to 80 days from plant to garment.

Nine slow steps — soak, mordant, cure, print, dry, wash, boil in copper, brighten with alum, finish. We never compress them. The wait is the work.

The Workshop 03

Made the slow way, on purpose.

Boiled in copper vessels, dried on grass under direct sun. Equipment that costs more, finishes that last longer, fabric that breathes.

The Wearer 04

Office at 9. Workshop in your closet.

Built for working women who measure clothing in years, not seasons. Soft on day one, deeper after every wash, hand-down ready in five.
